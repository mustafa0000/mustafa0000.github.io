World Arthritis Day: 12 October 2010
World Arthritis Day has been celebrated 1996 by ARI (Arthritis Rheumatism International) first. It is being celebrated each year on October 12th.

Now, people with arthritis from around the world join together to make their voices heard on this day. You can join in and raise this voice also!

The aims of World Arthritis Day are:
•To raise awareness of arthritis in all its forms among the medical community, people with arthritis and the general public.

•To influence public policy by making decision-makers aware of the burden of arthritis and the steps which can be taken to ease it.

•To ensure all people with arthritis and their caregivers are aware of the vast support network available to them.

SSR successfully conducted its World Arthritis Day in Riyadh

With more than 300 attendees, the Saudi Society of Rheumatology conducted its First World Arthritis Day in Riyadh city, center of Saudi Arabia.
This one day was for the public including Male, Female and Children .It was highly appreciated by patients community.
This activity was organized by Dr. Hanan Mohammad AL Rayes, the Chairperson of the SSR Educational committee, and 8 Rhematology Consultants from different Hospitals and tow physiotherapists conducted the day covering most aspects on Arthritis beginning with Types of Arthritis ,Overview of Rheumatoid Arthritis ,Life Style in Patients with Arthritis ,SLE, and Physiotherapy for patients with Arthritis..
Also, there was Free MSK US of the hand for the patients with RA, as well as free consultation and laboratory investigation.
